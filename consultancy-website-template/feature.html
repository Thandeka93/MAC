VISION, VALUES AND MISSION

OUR VISION

THL Global is a sprited and innovative engineering Company, our business journey is driven by the principle of serving and supporting all oue stakeholders. We are fully invested in our customers, our people, and communities that we operate in. We do building management systems (BMS) using Schneider Electric products.

Our unique approach is to develop a long -term partnership with our customers, optimizing solutions that fit our customers' needs. We have a full ambit of flexible engineering Solutions available, suitable for almost every requirement when it comes to gathering data, remote control/operation of equipment, and automation of reports that are supported by qualified professionals.

OUR VALUES

EXCELLENCE: At THL Global CC, we strive for excellence in everything we do. We are committed to delivering highquality products, services and solutions that meet and exceed our customers' expectations.

SAFETY: We are committed to maintaning a working environment that is safe and healthy for wellbeing of our employees and clients. Zero harm remains our ultimate objective, or immediate focus is on managing core risks and hazards to mitigate high-energy incidents.

CUSTOMER SATISFACTION: We prioritize the satisfaction of our customers above all else.We are dedicated to understanding their unique needs and providing tailored solutions that address their specific requirements. Our aim is to build long-term relationships based on trust, reliability, and exceptional service.

INNOVATION: We embrace innovation as a driving force behind our success. We continuosly explore new technologies, industry trends, and best practices to stay at the forefront of our field. By embracing innovation, we aim to provide our customers with cutting-edge solutions that optimize efficiency, sustainability, and perfomance.

SUSTAINABILITY: We are commited to promoting sustainability in the mining and electrical industry. Through the supply and installation of energy-effecient building management systems, we contribute to reducing environmental impact and fostering a greener future.

These values guide our actions and decisions at THL Global CC, shaping our company culture and driving towards success and positive impact.

OUR MISSION

The mission of THL Global CC is to revolutionize the mining and electrical industry by providing exceptional equipment, parts, and services while promoting diversity, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We strive to be a leader in the field, delivering innovative solutions that optimize efficiency, reduce environmental impact, and enhance the overall perfomance of our customers' operations.
